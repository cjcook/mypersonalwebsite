Welcome to my Website!

When I first started out as a developer I was really unsure of what to expect. I knew I was interested in computers and engineering, but I had no idea what computer science had to offer. After a few years of learning the basics of computer science, I began to understand and familiarize myself in certain areas of the field. One of the areas that I really struggled with at first was cloud computing. Cloud computing to me seemed impossible to learn. I would spend hours trying to figure out what was going on and what I could do to help me understand this topic more. As time went on I eventually got the hang of cloud computing. It took a quite some time to grasp, but when I realized that I was comfortable with the topic, I finally understood what computer science was all about. Computer Science takes time to understand, and you might not know what's going on at first in a specific topic until you thoroughly do research in that area.
